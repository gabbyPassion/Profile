About

I'm Gabrielle Beckford, a veterinarian turned Full Stack Software Developer. With over five years of diverse work experience in veterinary medicine, management, tech recruiting, and higher education, I bring a unique blend of skills to the table.

My journey into programming and development began in 2015 when I started a Wordpress travel blog. Through self-teaching, I learned how to utilize FTP servers, customize my WordPress site, and publish to the web. Initially, maintaining the blog was simply a hobby during college, and I never anticipated transitioning into a full-time career building websites and applications.

I have come to realize the immense opportunities to serve in the Tech Industry. Furthermore, I am inspired by its ability to address numerous societal pain points with significant impact and I am excited to be a part of it. Currently, I am on the verge of completing my Full Stack Web and Mobile Development certificate course. In the upcoming fall, I will commence my pursuit of a Computer Science BSc from a reputable online institution.
