About

I'm Gabrielle Beckford, a certified Full Stack Software Engineer. With eight years of diverse work experience including technical recruiting, higher education, veterinary medicine and management, I bring a unique blend of skills to the table.

My journey into programming and development began in 2015 when I started a Wordpress travel blog. Through self-teaching, I learned how to utilize FTP servers, customize my WordPress site, and publish to the web. What started as a college hobby unexpectedly paved the way for a transition from being a full-time veterinarian to a fulfilling career in website and software development.

I have recognized the immense opportunities to serve in the Tech Industry. Furthermore, I am inspired by its ability to address numerous societal pain points with significant impact and I am excited to be a part of it. Excited to contribute, I am currently pursuing a BSc in Computer Science on a part-time basis, underscoring my commitment to continual learning.
